Running OpenOA Examples¶

Jupyter notebooks demonstrating operational wind plant data analyses using OpenOA with example data stored in the ENTR warehouse are located at /examples in the ENTR OpenOA repository. All examples use two years of data for the 4-turbine “La Haute Borne” wind plant.
